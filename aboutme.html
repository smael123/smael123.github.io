Introduction

My name is Ismael Almaguer. I live in the United States in the state of Texas. I graduated from the University of Downtown (UHD) in 2017 with a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science (Much easier to say Computer Science degree).

Academic Life

At UHD I decided to major in Computer Science after being undeclared for approximately one year. I have always loved computers ever since my parents brought home a Windows ME computer from Circuit City. Ahh Circuit City. I will miss your red flooring that looked like the red brick road from the movie “The Wizard of Oz”.

Anyway, I took many Computer Science and Math courses in my academic life. When I took my first CS class at UHD I felt nervous because I thought that I would not understand the material. But as the course continued I was relieved to find that C++ was understandable and easy to implement a program with.

In school I took CS courses on Data Structures and Algorithms, Object Oriented Programming, Software Engineering, Databases, and Computer Graphics. I took Math courses on Calculus, Digital Logic, Discrete Mathematics, and Probability & Statistics.
